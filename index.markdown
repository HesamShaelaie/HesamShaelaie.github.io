Hesam is a Ph.D. student in the Department of [Industrial and Systems Engineering](https://engineering.lehigh.edu/ise) at [Lehigh University](https://www.lehigh.edu/). He is working with Prof. [Ted Ralphs](https://coral.ise.lehigh.edu/~ted/) and Prof. [Larry Snyder](https://engineering.lehigh.edu/faculty/lawrence-v-snyder). Hesam earned his B.Sc. (2013) and M.Sc. His current research interests include discrete, bilevel optimization, and analysis of algorithms for machine learning. To be more specific, he is interested in using machine learning techniques in discrete optimization.
